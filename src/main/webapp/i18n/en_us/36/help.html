House of Wild Prizes

Are you ready to play a Tic Tac Toe game? House of Wild Prizes lets you wipe the windows to collect the Chinese traditional paper-cut decorations.

Try to get 3 identical symbols on each horizontal, vertical and diagonal grid line to win a prize. If you get a "FU" symbol on the winning grid line, your winning prize might be boosted up to 4x.

How to Play

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

[top]

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

[top]

About The Game

The object of the game is to get three identical symbols or get two identical symbols and 1 prize multiplier symbol to win the associated prize.

Game Screen

After a ticket is purchased, the game displays a 3x3 Tic Tac Toe grid. Each grid cell contains a symbol or a prize multiplier.

Each horizontal, vertical and diagonal grid line is associated with a prize amount.

Winning a Prize

Select tiles or press REVEAL ALL button to reveal the symbols. If three identical symbols in a line are revealed, the prize associated with that line will be awarded.

If two identical symbols and a prize multiplier symbol in a line are revealed, the prize associated with that line will be multiplied by the value indicated by the prize multiplier symbol, and be awarded.

Game Completion

When all the windows are revealed, the game ends. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Wins are shown in currency.
